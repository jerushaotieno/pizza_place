About Us

Jay’s Pizza Joint was founded in 2003 by our founder Jay Reno. Since then, it has been serving delicious pizzas revered by everyone who visits the city. Jay’s Pizza Joint has been voted as the best pizza house in Nairobi for 10 consecutive years, a fete others can only dream of. We’ve also been voted as the best nationally 3 years in a row at the National Pizza Awards having come in at position 2 and 3 in the past on several occasions. We take our love for pizza seriously and our clients love us because they know that with us, all their pizza needs are met.
